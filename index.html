The Beginning :

“In 2008, I embarked on a transformative journey to improve my health and well-being, driven by dissatisfaction with my lifestyle. With the guidance of inspiring instructors, I gradually transformed my life through dedication to physical, mental, and emotional wellness. By 2010, my passion for yoga became a calling, leading me to share this life-changing practice with others. Over 15 years, teaching and practicing yoga has been a journey of growth, connection, and fulfillment. Beyond yoga, I am certified in Pilates, Power Yoga, and Kids Yoga, holding international credentials like RYS 200 and advanced certifications from Param Yoga. These skills enable me to offer holistic practices that foster balance and vitality. Grateful for this path, I remain motivated to empower individuals and organizations to embrace wellness and transform their lives.”

Our Commitment :

“At AstrovaFit, we inspire and empower individuals and organizations to achieve optimal health and well-being. Through transformative fitness solutions, personalized guidance, and innovative corporate wellness programs, we address unique challenges with tailored yoga classes and holistic strategies. Led by our renowned instructor, Pratima Vaidya, we strive to foster a culture of wellness that enables sustainable, positive lifestyle changes and promotes long-term vitality and balance.”

Key Services

  • High-Level Yoga Classes
  • Corporate Yoga Packages
Diverse Class Offerings

I conduct both online and offline yoga classes tailored to individuals and groups, ensuring accessibility and convenience. Each session is designed to cater to various skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

Holistic Workshops

I organize specialized yoga workshops focusing on themes like stress relief, flexibility, and mindfulness. These workshops are ideal for enhancing well-being and deepening one’s yoga practice.

Personalized Attention

Individual classes focus on personalized goals and specific needs for a customized yoga experience. Group classes foster a sense of community while maintaining a structured and inclusive environment.

Stress-Buster Package

A 4-week program featuring yoga sessions focused on stress management and relaxation techniques. Includes guided meditation and breathwork to help employees rejuvenate and enhance workplace productivity.

Energize & Align Package

Weekly yoga sessions designed to improve posture, flexibility, and overall physical wellness. Ideal for teams aiming to reduce sedentary fatigue and boost energy levels during work hours.

Wellness Retreat Package

A full-day or weekend retreat with yoga, mindfulness workshops, and team-building activities. This immersive experience helps employees reset and return to work with a renewed sense of focus and balance.
