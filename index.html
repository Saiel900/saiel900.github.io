About Pratima Vaidya

Pratima Vaidya is a passionate yoga instructor, Senior Corporate Wellness Consultant, and Spiritual Counselor dedicated to transforming lives through yoga, fitness, mindfulness, and discipline.

With 15+ years of wellness experience, she specializes in corporate and individual wellness programs, integrating yoga, mindfulness, spiritual counseling, and endurance training to create holistic transformation.

She has completed the TMM Half Marathon, actively participates in long-distance marathon events, and mentors elite runners in the Pune Running Group.
